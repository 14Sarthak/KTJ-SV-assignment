Radical optimism

Artists - Dua lipa , Chris Gehringer.

"Radical Optimism" is a vibrant exploration of psychedelic pop, marking a departure from the disco-centric sound of her previous album "Future Nostalgia." Released in May 2024, it's her third studio album and features collaborations with renowned artists like Chris gehringer. The album's title reflects Lipa's desire to infuse hope and optimism into the project, even amidst a world facing challenges.

Evolve

Artists- Imagine dragons, Den Reynolds.

Imagine Dragons' Evolve was a critical and commercial success, reaching number two on the Billboard 200 chart and achieving triple-platinum certification in the US. The album's shift towards a more pop-oriented sound, with electronic and R&B influences, was a notable change from their previous rock-heavy albums. Singles like "Believer," "Thunder," and "Whatever It Takes" became massive hits, solidifying the band's place as one of the biggest rock acts of the decade.
